Share your scans with KiP?

This sends your full scan history to the KiP research team to help us improve the app and build the I500 database.

What gets sent:

  • Barcodes and product names you scanned
  • Your traffic-light results
  • Decisions you made (Buy / Put back / Just looking)
  • Your selected health profile (diabetes, hypertension, etc.)

What doesn't get sent:

  • Your name, email, or phone number
  • Your location
  • Anything outside the app

You can send as many times as you want. Each send adds to the record; nothing gets deleted from your phone.
