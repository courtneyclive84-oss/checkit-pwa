How to Play

🎯 Your Mission

Big Label has infiltrated the food supply with fake "healthy" products. Swipe to match 3+ real foods and score points. Reach the target score before time runs out!

🍎 Real Food Tiles

Match 3 or more in a row/column to score. These are your allies.

⚠️ HMI Infiltrators

These mimic real food but are ultra-processed fakes. They look shinier and more appealing than natural tiles. If you match them, you lose a life and Dr. RooT reveals the real ingredients.

🔍 The Glitch Tell

Every 4 seconds, HMI tiles briefly glitch — flashing their true form for just 2 frames. Watch carefully! Paying attention is your best weapon.

🧑‍🔬 X-Ray Lens

Tap the X-RAY button to reveal all infiltrators for 3 seconds. You get 3 charges per level — use them wisely!

💀 UPF Blockers

Dark sludge tiles that can't be matched or moved. Tiles near them feel heavy and sticky to swipe. They appear from Level 3 onwards.

💀

❤️ 3 Lives

Each life = your Metabolic Integrity. Lose one every time an HMI fools you. At zero lives, it's game over. Dr. RooT will show you the real ingredient label of what you just "ate."

⏱️ The Timer

Represents your attention span in a world of marketing noise. It speeds up in later levels. When it hits 10 seconds, it pulses red.

🌟 Clarity Rating

At the end, you're scored on how many infiltrators you avoided, not just your points. Can you reach 100% clarity?
